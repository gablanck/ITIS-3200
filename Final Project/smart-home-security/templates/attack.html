ATTACK The moment this page loaded, a hidden form automatically sent a POST request to /device/lock/toggle on the Smart Home Dashboard — without you clicking anything.

Because your browser automatically attached your session cookie to that request, the server thought you sent it. Your door lock was just toggled by this fake website.

DEFENSE With SECURITY_ENABLED = True, Flask-WTF would have rejected this request immediately because the form has no CSRF token. The attacker cannot read your token (same-origin policy), so they cannot forge a valid request.

With SECURITY_ENABLED = False, the CSRF check is disabled and the attack succeeds — as you just saw.
